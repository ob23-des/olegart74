Features of the Vehicle

Toyota Corolla models are also very efficient (which means savings on fuel), are available in hybrid model configurations, and are easy and affordable to maintain.

Pricing

The 2024 Toyota Corolla has a starting sticker price of $23,145, with the range-topping Corolla XSE kicking off at $28,245.

Warranty

A 36-month or 36,000-mile bumper-to-bumper warranty that covers defects in materials or workmanship.
