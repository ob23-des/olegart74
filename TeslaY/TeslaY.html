Features of the Vehicle

The Model Y comes with forward collision warning and automatic emergency braking with pedestrian detection as standard features.

Pricing

The single-motor, rear-wheel drive model starts at $43,990. It comes with a long list of standard driver-assistance and infotainment features, and an EPA-estimated range of 260 miles.

Warranty

The Model Y has a basic vehicle warranty of 4 years or 50,000 miles, whichever comes first. The battery and drive unit warranty is 8 years or 120,000 miles, whichever comes first.
