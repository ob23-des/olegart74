Features of the Vehicle

Heated front seats, leather-wrapped steering wheel and shift knob, dual-zone automatic climate control, and a one-touch power moonroof.

Pricing

The starting price for the 2025 Honda CR-V is $30,100 for the base LX trim. The EX model starts at $32,350, and the EX-L starts at $35,000.

Warranty

A 3-year or 36,000-mile warranty that covers defects in materials or workmanship, and includes the 12-volt battery. It also includes 24-hour roadside assistance.
