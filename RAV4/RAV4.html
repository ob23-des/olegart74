Features of the Vehicle

The RAV4 comes standard with Toyota Safety Sense 2.5, which includes a pre-collision system, lane departure alert, adaptive cruise control, and more. Other safety features include blind spot monitor, rear cross-traffic alert, and automatic emergency braking with pedestrian detection.

Pricing

The 2024 Toyota RAV4 has a starting MSRP of $28,475, with prices ranging up to $39,645 for the top-of-the-line TRD Off-Road trim.

Warranty

Basic coverage includes: bumper-to-bumper warranty lasts for 36 months or 36,000 miles, whichever comes first. It covers defects in materials or workmanship, but not normal wear and maintenance.
