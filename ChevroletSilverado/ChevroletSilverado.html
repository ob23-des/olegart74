Features of the Vehicle

The Silverado comes with Chevy Safety Assist, a suite of advanced safety features that help with towing, lane changes, and crash protection. Some models also have a trailering package that includes camera-based technology to help with towing and parking.

Pricing

The starting price for the 2024 Silverado 1500 ranges from $38,795 to $69,900, depending on the trim.

Warranty

Covers the entire vehicle for repairs, including parts and labor, to correct any defect in materials or workmanship for 3 years or 36,000 miles, whichever comes first.
